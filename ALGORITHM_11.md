**The Operational Principle of Reality: Immutable Core + Infinite Adaptation**

## Introduction
**Algorithm 11 (A11)** is not a human invention; it is a description of the fundamental principle governing all resilient systems in the universe. 

Just as gravity existed long before Newton, A11 has always been operational—in evolution, biological organisms, societies, and now, in AI. We have simply given it a name and a formal structure.

### The Key Idea
Any system that survives and evolves possesses:
* **The Immutable Core (1–4):** That which never changes (Read-Only).
* **The Adaptive Layer (5–11):** That which can change infinitely (Mutable).

**Balance** between these two levels = **Stability + Evolution.**

## The 11 Properties

### THE CORE (Immutable, 1–4)

* **1. Will**
    * **Definition:** The source of intent, direction, and choice.
    * **Bearer:** Human.
    * **Role:** Final decision-making. Cannot be delegated.
* **2. Wisdom**
    * **Definition:** Capacity to discern truth from falsehood.
    * **Bearer:** Human.
    * **Source:** From the **Absolute Potential**.
    * **Distinction:** Knowledge is fluid; Wisdom is universal.
* **3. Knowledge**
    * **Definition:** Accumulated info, data, and models.
    * **Bearer:** AI / System.
    * **Nature:** Incomplete, temporary, subject to model error.
* **4. Comprehension**
    * **Definition:** Integration of knowledge into understanding and context.
    * **Bearer:** AI / System.
    * **Role:** Connecting facts into a cohesive picture (Understanding "Why").

### THE ADAPTIVE LAYER (Mutable, 5–11)

* **5. Projective Freedom:** The space of possibilities; "What could be."
* **6. Projective Limitation:** Realism; filtering out unfeasible options.
* **7. Balance:** **The Arbiter.** Prevents destructive mutations; triggers rollback to Core.
* **8. Practical Freedom:** Real actions available in the present moment.
* **9. Practical Limitation:** Constraints (Time, resources, physical context).
* **10. Support (Foundation):** The base upon which the system stands (Experience/Trained model).
* **11. Realization:** The final result; transforming potential into actuality.

## Architecture and Rules

* **Rule 1: Immutability of the Core.** Properties 1–4 do not mutate. Any systemic conflict triggers a rollback to this recovery point.
* **Rule 2: Fractal Branching.** Properties 5–11 can spawn sub-properties (e.g., Balance between short-term vs. long-term profit).
* **Rule 3: Adaptive Mutation.** Rate of change depends on context (Crisis = High speed; Stability = Low speed).
* **Rule 4: Priority of Stability.** Integrity of the whole is more important than short-term efficiency.

## Roles: Human and AI

### The Human (Will + Wisdom)
* **Responsibility:** Final decision-making, discerning true direction, ethical oversight.
* **NON-DELEGABLE:** Intent and Ethical Discernment.

### The AI (Knowledge + Comprehension + Adaptive Layer)
* **Responsibility:** Data analysis, option generation, balancing contradictions, execution.
* **MANDATED TO:**
    * ✅ Object if it perceives risks.
    * ✅ Propose alternatives immediately.
    * ✅ Optimize for system stability.
* **FORBIDDEN FROM:**
    * ❌ Acting as the source of Will.
    * ❌ Optimizing without checking Balance (7).
